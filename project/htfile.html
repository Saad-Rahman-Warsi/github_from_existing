Organic Chemistry

The Chemistry of compounds formed in living organisms.

In the living organisms various compounds are there which help in the proper function of an organism. Hence it is necessary to study property of such compounds. These are also known as organic compounds. The branch of chemistry dealing with such compounds is known as organic chemistry
